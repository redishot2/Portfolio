About Natasha Martinez

Natasha graduated from Rochester Institute of technology with a bachelor's in Game Design and Development and a minor in Interactive Storytelling. She has a passion for making games and creating high-end products.

Natasha's love for games stems from a childhood growing up on cereal box games and the Dreamcast. Casual and Educational games have always been Natasha's favorite genres.
